1 Who We Are

MY University is a digital educational platform developed and operated by Google Antigravity ("we", "us", "our", or "the Company"). The platform provides engineering students — primarily within Bangladesh — with course content, video lectures, academic schedules, community tools, and related services through our mobile application (Android & iOS) and web interface.

References to "MY University", "the App", or "the Platform" in this document refer to all products and services operated by Google Antigravity under the MY University brand.

2 Scope & Acceptance

This Privacy Policy applies to all users of MY University, including registered students, class representatives (CRs), administrators, and any visitors to our web platform.

By registering an account, accessing the platform, or continuing to use MY University after this policy takes effect, you explicitly acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to this Privacy Policy in its entirety. If you do not agree, you must discontinue use of the platform immediately and request account deletion.

This policy should be read alongside our Terms of Service, which together form the complete legal agreement between you and the Company.

7 Sensitive Data — Blood Donation Feature

The blood donation feature collects health-related information, specifically blood group and medical urgency details. We treat this data with heightened care:

  • Blood group information is stored separately and only displayed when you opt-in as a blood donor.
  • Your phone number is only shared with a verified requester when you explicitly choose to respond to a donation request.
  • Blood donation requests submitted on behalf of a patient may include the patient's contact information — you are solely responsible for obtaining the patient's consent before submitting such information.
  • We do not use blood group data for any purpose other than operating the blood donation feature.
  • You may remove your blood group and donor status from your profile at any time.

9 Security Measures

We implement technical and organizational measures designed to protect your data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction:

  • Encryption in transit: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using TLS 1.2 or higher.
  • Encryption at rest: Database data stored in Supabase (PostgreSQL) and Firestore is encrypted at rest by default.
  • Password security: Passwords are never stored in plain text. Firebase Authentication manages credential hashing using industry-standard algorithms.
  • Access controls: Access to production data is restricted to authorized personnel only, using role-based access control (RBAC).
  • Crash monitoring: Firebase Crashlytics is used to detect and respond to security-related app failures in real time.
  • Network security: The Android app enforces a strict Network Security Configuration that restricts cleartext HTTP traffic.

No security system is 100% impenetrable. While we take every reasonable precaution, we cannot guarantee absolute security. In the event of a data breach that affects your personal data, we will notify affected users as required by applicable law.
